Site Plan: The Daily Insight

Author: Olalemi Emmanuel

Site Purpose

The Daily Insight is a modern digital newspaper offering timely news, in-depth analysis, and opinion pieces across politics, business, technology, and culture. It aims to inform, engage, and empower readers with credible journalism and diverse perspectives.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest headlines in Nigerian politics?
  • Where can I find expert analysis on global economic trends?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #1a1a1a — used for text and headers

Accent Color: #c62828 — used for links and highlights

Background Color: #f5f5f5 — used for page background

Typography

Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
